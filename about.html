Hello there!

My name is Karina Roman. I'm an artist, design enthusiast, and pattern lover born and raised in New York. My love for art stems from my parents Colombian culture. I have a BA in Studio Art from Hunter College, and I'm currently pursuing my second BFA in Interaction Design at Queens College.

I decided to dive deeper into the design field because I fell in love with the idea of creating meaningful and relevant experiences for users through design. I'm currently focused on using design to create change and help creatively address the needs of others.
